"I made this tier list for the purpose placing the units of the game in order from best to worst in a way that is beneficial to players unsure of various units or to get the most of their options. This tier list also works as a sort of encyclopedia for Blazing units. **Tip**: If you are looking for a certain character, use Control + F (Windows) or Command + F (Mac) and type in your character's name, number or title. " ## How does this work ? The tier list currently divided into 5 sections (Heart, Skill, Body, Bravery & Wisdom) and 3 sub-sections (F2P, P2P & BBF).
